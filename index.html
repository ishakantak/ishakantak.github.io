About Me

I am currently an undergraduate student at York University. I am majoring in Honours BSC Computer Science

The thing that drew me to computer science is that I always wanted to know how something is built. I always wanted to tear apart an object and put it back together.

Then when I started to use computers for school work and to play games. I wanted to know how websites, applications and games were created.

In high school, I decided to take an elective computer science class. This is where I found a way to recreate a game or create a website. That is when I decided computer science was the right path for me.

In my past time I like to bake, read books and do embroiddery. Also I sometimes go to hackathons and I am also part of a club called ExcelLassonde.
